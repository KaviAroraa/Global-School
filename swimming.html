Swimming

  • Swimming is useful to children in many ways.

  • It not only strengthens the shoulder and thigh muscles, but also allows children to breathe deeply, and sustain breath under water for long periods of time.

  • Realizing the importance of swimming for fun and as a sport, children are provided specific time slots and weekend slots for practice of swimming.

  • In GIIS, swimming is conducted by trained coaches and is supervised carefully for the safety of each and every student involved in the activity.
